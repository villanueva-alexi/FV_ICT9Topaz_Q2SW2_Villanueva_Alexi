About the Lessons

Lessons Description
POETRY The topics learned under poetry will be: Elements of Poetry, Types of Poetry, Delving into Themes, and more.

Students will engage in activities such as: Poetry Festivals, Slam Poetry Recital, Collaborative Writing, and Poetry Showcasing
IMPROVISATION Students will engage in activities which will enhance their ability to improvise such as: Character Swaps, Group Storytelling, ABC Improv, Genre Switch, and more.

These activities will enhance the students' ability to response when unexpected situations arise and more.
THEATRE The topics learned under theatre will be: Method Acting, Character Development, Voice and Diction, Theatre History, Playwriting, Stagecraft, and Directing.

Students will engage in activities such as: Character Monologue Recitation, Short Plays, Emotion Charades, School Theatre Performances, Role-Playing, and more.
MUSICAL THEATRE Students will learn and do the following: Vocal Lessons, Acting Lessons, Musical Theatre Repertoire, and more.
